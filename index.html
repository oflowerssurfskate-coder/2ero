2ero 自幼学习小提琴，古典训练的根基让他对声音有着敏锐的感知。而电子音乐的出现，让他重新建立了对声音、空间与感知的理解。

他的音乐实践根植于 Leftfield Electronic Music、Bass Culture 与 Experimental Club Sounds，在 Techno、Bass Music、IDM、Dub 与 Ambient 之间自由游走。

作为 OFlowers 与 OFlowers Surfskate Club 的创始人，他长期致力于推动运动文化、电子音乐与青年社群之间的融合发展。

Raised through years of classical violin training, 2ero discovered electronic music as a way to reshape his understanding of sound, space and perception. His practice draws from Leftfield Electronic Music, Bass Culture and Experimental Club Sounds — moving freely between Techno, Bass Music, IDM, Dub and Ambient. As the founder of OFlowers and OFlowers Surfskate Club, he focuses on connecting movement, music and contemporary youth culture through community-driven projects and events.
